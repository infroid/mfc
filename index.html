FAQ

Common questions

What health data does MFC use?

MFC can read from Apple Health, Google Fit, or manual entries. We look at blood work markers (iron, B12, vitamin D, cholesterol), vitals (weight, blood pressure, heart rate), and your stated dietary goals and restrictions.

Do I need a fitness tracker?

No. You can enter your health data manually. A connected tracker just makes it automatic and keeps your profile updated in real time.

How are meals personalized?

We combine your health metrics, nutritional gaps, taste preferences, allergies, and cultural food habits to suggest meals that are both healthy for you and enjoyable to eat.

Is my health data safe?

Yes. All health data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never sell or share your data. Your metrics are used only to personalize your experience.

Can I follow my own recipes?

Absolutely. You can add your own recipes and MFC will analyze them for nutritional content, suggest tweaks if needed, and add them to your guided cooking library.

Is MFC free?

MFC is free to get started with core features. Premium features like advanced health analytics and detailed micronutrient tracking are available on our paid plans.
